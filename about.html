About

Cozy Arcade exists to host small, calm games you can play in your browser. Nothing more, nothing less.

The games here are meant to be quiet experiences—no pressure, no competition, no accounts. Just something to do for a few minutes when you need a moment of calm.

This isn't a platform or a community. It's not trying to grow or become something bigger. It's just a small collection of games that felt worth sharing.

If you're looking for something polished or ambitious, you might be disappointed. These are simple games, made with care but without grand ambitions.
