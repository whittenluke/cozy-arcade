How to play

  • Build a path of valid words from Start to End.
  • First word must touch Start; a word must eventually touch End to win.
  • Each new word must connect to letters already on the board.
  • Where your word touches existing letters, those crossings must form valid words too.
  • Pick a cell, choose direction (→ or ↓), select tiles in order, then Place.
